# Stranger test — tester pack

Hand this to 3 people who have never seen Everion. Aim: find what's confusing
before strangers do at scale. Total time: ~15 min per person.

## What I need from you

- Pick **any email** you can check right now (Gmail, work, throwaway — your choice).
- **Start a screen recording.** Talk out loud as you go. "What is this?", "Why does it want…", "Where do I…" — those are the gold.
- **Don't ask me anything.** Pretend I'm not in the room. If you're stuck, you're stuck — note it and move on.
- Stop after 15 minutes even if you didn't finish. The stuck points are the data.

When you're done, send me:

1. The screen recording
2. The 4 wrap-up answers below
3. Anything else you noticed

→ stander.christian@gmail.com

---

## Setup (1 min)

1. Open https://everion.smashburgerbar.co.za on your phone or laptop — your choice
2. Hit record on your screen recorder

## Tasks (10 min)

Don't peek ahead. One at a time. Speak out loud throughout.

1. **Look at the first screen for 10 seconds.** Don't click anything. Say out loud what you think this app does and who it's for.
2. **Sign up.** Use the email you picked.
3. **Save something you'd actually want to remember** — anything. A thought, a quote, a thing on your mind, whatever.
4. **Find what you just saved.** Without using the back button.
5. **Ask the app a question about what you saved.** Anything you'd want it to tell you.
6. **Open Settings.** Change anything you want — your name, the theme, whatever catches your eye.
7. **Sign out, then sign back in.** Just to see what that's like.

## Wrap-up (3 min)

Type your answers, no need to be polite:

1. **What was confusing?** (List as many as you remember.)
2. **What did you expect that wasn't there?**
3. **In one sentence, what would you actually use this for?**
4. **0–10, how likely are you to recommend this to a friend?** Why that number?

## What I'm watching for (don't read this beforehand — for the host)

Friction signals when reviewing the recording:

- Hesitation before any click >3s
- Going back to a previous screen
- Hovering on something they didn't click
- Re-reading a label or button
- Verbal: "what's this?", "I don't know what to put", "where's…"
- Bailing on a task

Severity calls:

- **Blocker** — they couldn't complete the task. Fix before launch.
- **Friction** — they completed it but were confused. Fix before scale.
- **Polish** — finished cleanly but mentioned it. Backlog.

Aim for 3 testers because that's where you stop hearing new things — if all 3 stumble on the same step, that step is broken, not them.
