# OpenBrain — Starter Questions

> The 30 most important things to store in your second brain on day one. These are the details you'll desperately need someday and won't have — identity numbers, emergency contacts, medical info, financial details, and the serial numbers you'll wish you'd saved. Work through all 30 to build a solid foundation.

---

### 1. Identity Document
What is your national ID or social security number? Include your full legal name, date of birth, and document issue date.

### 2. Passport
What is your passport number, country of issue, issue date, and expiry date? If you hold dual citizenship, include both.

### 3. Driver's Licence
What is your licence number, category/class, issue date, and expiry date? Where was it issued?

### 4. Blood Type
What is your blood type? (If you don't know, this is worth finding out — it could save your life in an emergency.)

### 5. Allergies
Do you have any allergies — medications, foods, insect stings, latex, environmental? List all of them, even mild ones.

### 6. Emergency Contacts
Who should be called in a medical emergency? List 2-3 people with their names, relationship to you, and phone numbers.

### 7. Medical Insurance
Who is your health insurance provider? Include your policy/member number, the type of plan, and the emergency and general contact numbers.

### 8. Primary Doctor
Who is your GP or primary care physician? Name, practice name, phone number, and address.

### 9. Chronic Conditions & Medication
Do you take any chronic medication? List each medication, the dosage, frequency, and what it's prescribed for.

### 10. Next of Kin
Who is your legal next of kin? Full name, relationship, phone number, and address. Is this documented anywhere official?

### 11. Home Address
What is your full residential address including postal/zip code? If you have a separate mailing address, include that too.

### 12. Vehicle Details
What vehicle do you drive? Make, model, year, colour, registration/licence plate number, and VIN (usually on a sticker inside the driver's door frame).

### 13. Vehicle Insurance
Who is your vehicle insurance provider? Policy number, monthly premium, and claims contact number.

### 14. Roadside Assistance
Do you have roadside assistance? Provider, membership number, and emergency call-out number.

### 15. Bank Account
What bank is your primary account with? Account type, branch/routing code, and the bank's fraud/emergency line. (Don't store your account number here — just enough to identify and contact.)

### 16. Tax Number
What is your tax identification number? Include the relevant tax authority and your filing frequency (annual, quarterly).

### 17. Accountant or Tax Advisor
Who handles your taxes? Name, firm, phone number, and email.

### 18. Lawyer or Legal Advisor
Do you have a lawyer? Name, firm, speciality, phone number, and email. Even if you don't need one now, you will someday.

### 19. Will & Testament
Do you have a will? Where is the original stored? Who is the executor? When was it last updated?

### 20. Home Insurance
Do you have home/renters insurance? Provider, policy number, and what's covered? Where is the policy document stored?

### 21. Phone & Laptop
What phone and laptop do you use? For each: brand, model, serial number, purchase date, and warranty expiry. These details are needed for insurance claims and repairs.

### 22. Internet & Utilities
Who is your internet service provider? Account number and monthly cost. Same for electricity, water, and gas if applicable.

### 23. Alarm & Home Security
Do you have a home alarm or security system? Provider, account number, armed response number, and — store a hint for — your alarm code.

### 24. Important Document Storage
Where do you keep your critical physical documents? (Birth certificate, marriage certificate, property deeds, academic certificates, insurance policies.) Name the location.

### 25. Employer or Business Details
If employed: company name, employee number, HR contact, and work address. If self-employed: business registration number, trading name, and registered address.

### 26. Children or Dependants
Do you have children or dependants? Names, dates of birth, ID/passport numbers, schools, and any medical details worth storing (allergies, blood type, doctor).

### 27. Pet Details
Do you have pets? Names, breed, microchip number, vet name and number, vaccination schedule, and any medications.

### 28. Key Dates & Anniversaries
List the birthdays and anniversaries you must never forget — partner, parents, children, best friends. Include the year if you want to track ages.

### 29. Spare Keys & Access
Where are your spare keys stored — house, car, office? Does anyone else hold a copy? Who?

### 30. Subscriptions & Recurring Payments
List your active subscriptions and monthly costs: streaming services, gym, cloud storage, insurance premiums, software licences, meal kits — everything that auto-debits. You'll be shocked how much it adds up to.

---

*Once you've answered these 30, you have a safety net for life's worst moments and a foundation to build on. Everything else — recipes, ideas, contacts, goals — is a bonus on top of this core.*
