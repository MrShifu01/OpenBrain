# Hunter outreach — Product Hunt launch (~2026-07-01)

**One yes is enough.** A hunter with PKM/productivity karma posts your launch, their followers see it, PH amplifies it in feed-rank. Hunter-led launches outperform self-hunted launches 3-10x.

**Send 5-10 of these in week 6 of beta phase (~2026-06-15 to 2026-06-20).** Personalise every one — generic copy-paste reads as bot. The 30 minutes you spend reading their last 3 hunts and their last 5 tweets is the difference between "thanks but no" and "yes, I'll hunt this."

---

## Candidate criteria

A good hunter for Everion Mind has at least 3 of these:

- [ ] **PH karma rank** in the top ~500 (visible on their profile). They've posted launches that ranked top 10.
- [ ] **Hunted PKM/note-taking apps before** — Reflect, Mem, Notion, Tana, Obsidian, Capacities, Heptabase, Roam, Logseq, Saga, Anytype.
- [ ] **Engaged audience** ≥ 5K Twitter followers / ≥ 10K newsletter / ≥ 20K LinkedIn. Not a vanity number — comments on their posts > 5% of follower count.
- [ ] **Active in PKM discourse** — tweets/blogs about second brains, Zettelkasten, journaling, knowledge work.
- [ ] **Has hunted a launch in the last 90 days.** Inactive hunters' followers stop opening their notifications.
- [ ] **Hunter ≠ direct competitor founder.** Don't ask the Mem founder to hunt Everion. Asking the Mem *user community* curator is fine.

## Where to find them

1. PH leaderboard: `producthunt.com/leaderboard/community` — sort by All-time. Filter to people who've launched/hunted in productivity.
2. Recent PKM launches on PH: open the launch, click the hunter, check their profile, repeat.
3. Twitter search: `from:* second brain min_faves:50` and look at who replies/retweets the popular ones.
4. Indie Hackers: search "Product Hunt" + "second brain" — finds people who write about hunting these specifically.

## DM / email template (~140 words — short on purpose)

```
Subject: would you hunt Everion Mind on PH?

Hi {{first_name}},

I've been following your work on {{specific_thing_they_did_recently}} —
{{1-sentence-genuine-reaction}}.

I'm launching Everion Mind on Product Hunt around July 1st.

It's a second brain with two halves: a memory feed for the fleeting stuff
(half-thoughts, voice memos, links) and an AES-256 encrypted vault for the
high-stakes stuff (ID numbers, gate codes, "if I die" notes). You ask either
half in plain language and it cites the entries it answered from. PWA +
Android live; iOS in the App Store the same week.

Would you be open to hunting it? Happy to send a 60-second demo. Lifetime
Pro on me regardless.

Either way, thank you for the work — it's seeded a lot of how I think about
this kind of tool.

— Christian
```

### How to fill the placeholders

- `{{first_name}}` — their actual first name. Not "hi there." Not "hi maker."
- `{{specific_thing_they_did_recently}}` — pick from their last 5 tweets or their last hunt. Examples: "the way you framed Tana as 'spreadsheet-shaped Zettelkasten' last Thursday", "your post on why most second-brain tools die at the 50-entry threshold", "the Mem launch comment about extraction vs ingestion."
- `{{1-sentence-genuine-reaction}}` — what was your honest takeaway. NOT "great post!" — something that proves you read it. "Made me re-think how we're surfacing the gap-analyst output."

**If you can't fill all three placeholders for a candidate, don't send to them.** The point is to prove you've done the work to deserve a yes.

## What to attach / link

- **60-second demo video** (the one from Week 2 day 12 of the schedule) — Loom or YouTube unlisted. NOT autoplay-blocked.
- **Live PWA URL** — let them tap around. PH hunters mostly want to verify the product is real before they put their name on it.
- **One-line tagline** — "your second brain — kept quietly."
- **PH "upcoming" page link** — once it's live (~day 35).

## Follow-up cadence

- **Day 0:** send the DM
- **Day 4:** if no response, ONE follow-up: "no worries if not a fit — just bumping in case the first message got buried." Then drop it.
- **Day 7:** mark as "no" and move to the next candidate. Do not third-message.

## What to NEVER do

- Mass-send the same template — PH/Twitter community is small, hunters talk
- Buy upvotes or pay for PH "promotion" — gets you shadow-banned
- Ask a hunter who already hunted a direct competitor in the last 6 months — conflict of interest, they'll say no
- Promise revenue share — looks transactional, breaks the gift-economy norm of PH culture
- Email a hunter you've never engaged with on social — cold outreach to indie influencers has near-zero hit rate

## What to do AFTER they say yes

1. Send them the demo + live URL within 24h
2. Confirm launch date (Tuesday-Thursday, NOT Monday/Friday)
3. Send them: tagline, description, asset (1280×720 GIF/MP4), maker comment draft (so they know what tone to set)
4. Add them as the first user of Everion Mind, with Pro tier flipped manually via AdminTab override
5. Day before launch: send a short message recapping plan + your timezone + your phone number for emergencies
6. Day of launch: be available within 30 min of every comment — they're putting their name on this
