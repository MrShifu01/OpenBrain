## "Notify me" funnel — how to convert PH visitors

PH visitors who click "Notify me" are the highest-quality leads in your funnel:

- **Higher intent** than a Twitter follower (they actively asked for an email)
- **Aware of launch timing** (PH controls the email-on-launch-day flow)
- **Likely to upvote** (they came for THIS launch specifically)

Don't spam them. PH sends ONE email on launch day automatically — that's the contract. Don't add them to your general newsletter without their separate consent.

If you want to nurture, the only acceptable touch is:

- **Day -3 (Friday before launch):** ONE Twitter post tagged with the PH "upcoming" page asking "What's the one feature you'd want me to highlight?" — invites engagement, signals momentum to PH ranking algorithm.

That's it. Everything else compounds via PH's own email and your daily Twitter cadence.

## What NOT to put on the "upcoming" page

- ❌ Generic AI-app copy ("Powered by GPT-4" / "AI-powered productivity")
- ❌ Vague mission statements ("We believe in...")
- ❌ Pricing — PH culture treats pricing on the upcoming page as off-putting; save it for launch day
- ❌ Testimonials before launch — feels staged
- ❌ "Built with [tech stack]" — irrelevant to PH audience, save for HN
- ❌ Roadmap / "coming soon" feature lists — sells a future product, hurts conversion

The whole "upcoming" page is one promise: **on day X, this will exist, and it does this one specific thing.** Anything that distracts from that hurts conversion.
